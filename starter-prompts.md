# Template Starter Prompts

> Punchy one-liners to kick off building each template.

## Record-to-Report

| Template | Starter Prompt |
|----------|----------------|
| Month-End Close Checklist | "Build me a close checklist that tracks tasks across my team and auto-generates flux narratives" |
| Intercompany Reconciliation | "Help me match intercompany transactions across entities and generate elimination entries" |
| Journal Entry Automation | "Create an app that reads my accrual schedules and auto-generates monthly journal entries" |
| Flux Analysis & Variance Reporter | "Build a variance analyzer that explains why my numbers changed in plain English" |
| Fixed Asset Tracker | "Track my fixed assets, calculate depreciation, and generate the monthly entries" |
| Lease Accounting (ASC 842) | "Extract lease terms from contracts and calculate my ASC 842 entries automatically" |

## Order-to-Cash

| Template | Starter Prompt |
|----------|----------------|
| Billing & Revenue Recognition | "Build a billing system that generates invoices and calculates ASC 606 rev rec schedules" |
| Collections Agent | "Create a collections agent that chases overdue invoices and escalates to Slack" |
| Cash Application | "Match incoming payments to open invoices using AI and flag exceptions" |
| Contract Review & Revenue Impact | "Analyze new contracts and flag terms that affect revenue recognition" |
| Deferred Revenue Waterfall | "Track my deferred revenue by contract and generate waterfall reports for the board" |

## Procure-to-Pay

| Template | Starter Prompt |
|----------|----------------|
| Invoice Processing & Coding | "Process incoming invoices, auto-code to GL accounts, and route for approval" |
| Procurement Compliance Agent | "Check every PO against our policies and flag violations before they post" |
| Vendor Onboarding & Risk | "Build a vendor onboarding portal that collects W-9s and screens against sanctions lists" |
| Three-Way Match | "Match POs to receipts to invoices and auto-approve within tolerance" |
| Spend Analytics | "Show me where we're spending money and flag anomalies like duplicate payments" |

## Treasury & Cash Management

| Template | Starter Prompt |
|----------|----------------|
| Cash Flow Forecasting | "Generate a 13-week cash forecast from my AR, AP, and payroll data" |
| Bank Reconciliation | "Reconcile my bank transactions to the GL and flag unmatched items" |
| Intercompany Cash Management | "Track intercompany loans, calculate interest, and generate settlement entries" |

## People & Payroll

| Template | Starter Prompt |

## Tax & Compliance

## FP&A & Strategic Finance

| Template | Starter Prompt |
|----------|----------------|

| Board Reporting Package | "Assemble my KPIs and financials into a board-ready deck with AI-written narratives" |
