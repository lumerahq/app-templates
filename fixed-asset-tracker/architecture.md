## Overview

Track fixed assets, run monthly depreciation (straight-line or declining balance), view schedules, handle disposals with gain/loss.

## Collections

| Collection | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `fat_assets` | Asset master — cost, useful life, method, category |
| `fat_depreciation_entries` | Monthly depreciation records |
| `fat_disposals` | Asset disposal records with gain/loss |
